# CLID – Community Leadership Inclusion and Development

CLID is a charitable organization committed to building inclusive communities through human rights advocacy, social justice initiatives, and empowered community leadership. This repository contains the source code for the CLID website.

## Vision
Inclusive communities. Empowered lives.

To build an inclusive society where every individual, regardless of background or status, has equal access to justice, opportunity, and a dignified life.

## Mission
Advancing justice, leadership, and opportunity for all through grassroots empowerment and advocacy.

CLID promotes inclusive development by fostering community leadership, providing legal and social support services, and empowering marginalized populations through education, collaboration, and sustained advocacy.

## About the Website
The CLID website serves as:
- An information hub for CLID’s mission, values, and programs  
- A platform for advocacy, education, and community engagement  
- A point of contact for partners, volunteers, and beneficiaries  

## Contributing
Contributions are welcome and should align with CLID’s mission and values of inclusion, equity, and social justice.
