Welcome to CLID

Building inclusive communities through justice, leadership, and opportunity. We work with communities to strengthen leadership, advance human rights, and create pathways to dignity and inclusion for all.

Community Leadership Inclusion and Development (CLID) is a charitable organization focused on promoting social justice, human rights, and inclusive development. We believe sustainable change begins when communities are empowered to lead, advocate, and shape their own futures. Through education, legal and social support, and grassroots collaboration, CLID works to address systemic barriers and expand access to opportunity for marginalized populations.
