Get Involved

Creating inclusive communities requires collective effort. Whether you want to contribute your time, skills, or support, there are many ways to be part of the work CLID is doing.

Be Part of the Change

CLID is built on the belief that meaningful change happens when people come together with a shared purpose. Every contribution — no matter how small — plays a role in expanding opportunity, strengthening communities, and promoting justice.

Ways to Get Involved

Volunteer With Us

Contribute your time and skills to support community initiatives, educational programs, and outreach efforts. Volunteering with CLID is an opportunity to make a direct impact while gaining valuable experience.

Become a Volunteer

Partner With Us

We collaborate with organizations, institutions, and community groups to expand our reach and strengthen our impact. Partnerships allow us to deliver more effective and sustainable solutions.

Become a Partner

Support Our Work

Your support helps us continue providing resources, advocacy, and opportunities to those who need them most. Contributions enable us to expand our programs and reach more communities.

Support CLID

Be an Advocate

Help raise awareness about issues affecting marginalized communities. By sharing knowledge, engaging in conversations, and supporting inclusive initiatives, you contribute to broader social change.

Learn How to Advocate

Who Can Get Involved

We welcome individuals and organizations from all backgrounds who are committed to promoting inclusion, justice, and community development.

  • Students and young professionals
  • Community members
  • Organizations and institutions
  • Advocates for social justice

What You Gain

Getting involved with CLID is not only about giving — it is also about growth, connection, and shared purpose.

  • Opportunities to contribute to meaningful change
  • Experience in community-focused initiatives
  • Exposure to real-world social impact work
  • Connection with like-minded individuals

How It Works

  1. Express your interest
  2. We connect with you
  3. You get involved in ongoing initiatives

Take the First Step

If you’re ready to contribute, collaborate, or support, we’d love to hear from you. Every effort counts, and every voice matters.
